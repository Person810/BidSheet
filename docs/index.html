Built for underground utility contractors

Everything a small subcontractor needs to estimate and bid work, nothing they don't.

Bid Proposals

Build construction estimates organized by section and line item. Auto-calculate totals with overhead, profit, bond, and tax markups. Generate professional bid PDFs to hand to general contractors.

Plan Takeoff

Built-in PDF plan viewer with per-page scale calibration, drawing overlay, and pipe run creation. Takeoff quantities flow directly into your bid line items.

Trench Profiler

Calculate excavation and backfill volumes for pipe trenches. Input pipe size, depth, width, and bedding — volumes roll up into bid line items automatically.

Material Catalog

Maintain material pricing with history tracking. Import supplier price sheets via CSV with fuzzy matching. Includes 600+ seed items for water, sewer, storm, gas, and fiber trades.

Crews & Labor

Build crew templates with burdened labor rates and production rates. Track equipment costs for owned and rented machines. Assign crews to bid line items.

Win/Loss Tracking

Track bid outcomes to sharpen your estimating over time. Duplicate previous bids as starting points. Lock won bids to protect the record. Manage change orders.

Stop estimating in Excel

BidSheet is free, always will be. If you're pricing water mains, sewer lines, or storm drain in a spreadsheet, this is for you.
